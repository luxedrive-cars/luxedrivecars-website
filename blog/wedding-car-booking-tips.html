5 Tips for Booking Your Wedding Hire in Melbourne

Your wedding car is one of the easier bookings to put off - until you realise everyone else's wedding car is booked for your date too. Here's what actually matters when locking one in.

1. Book earlier than feels necessary

Popular wedding dates and weekends in peak season can book out months in advance. If you've already locked in a venue and date, it's worth enquiring about your car at the same time, not after everything else is sorted.

2. Plan your timing around the whole day, not just the ceremony

Think through every leg: getting to the ceremony, photos afterward, and the trip to your reception. Share your full schedule when you enquire so timing can be planned around it properly, not just a single pickup.

3. Confirm exactly who's travelling in the car

Is it just the couple, or the full bridal party too? This affects which vehicle in a fleet actually suits your day, so it's worth confirming early rather than assuming.

4. Ask about decoration options

If you want ribbon, florals, or other styling on the car, ask whether that's something the provider can arrange or whether you'll need to organise it yourself ahead of time.

5. Have a backup plan for weather and timing blowouts

Photos run long, weather turns - ask how flexible your booking is if the day runs over, so it's not a surprise on the day itself.
