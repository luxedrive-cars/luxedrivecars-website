Airport Transfer vs Rideshare: What's the Difference?

When you're heading to or from Melbourne Airport, it's tempting to just open a rideshare app. But there are a few real differences worth knowing about before your next trip.

Pricing certainty

Rideshare pricing fluctuates with demand - surge pricing during peak travel times can make a "cheap" trip surprisingly expensive. A pre-arranged chauffeur transfer is quoted upfront.

No waiting in the queue

At busy times, airport rideshare pickup zones can mean a long wait after you've already been travelling for hours. A pre-booked chauffeur is there waiting for you.

Flight tracking

A booked transfer service can track your flight and adjust pickup timing if you're delayed - a rideshare driver can't.

The vehicle and the experience

Rideshare is whatever car happens to be nearby. A chauffeur service like LuxeDrive Cars means arriving in a presented manner, with a professional driver, every time.
